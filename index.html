Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program(sinp) Calculator

The Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program (SINP) uses a unique Expression of Interest (EOI) points grid to rank candidates in its Saskatchewan Express Entry and Occupations In-Demand sub-categories. To qualify, candidates are required to score a minimum of 60 points out of 100 in the SINP point assessment grid.

Points are calculated based on the information provided by the client. Can X Immigration & Consulting Inc. does not hold any responsibility form getting an ITA from SINP.
